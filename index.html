RockPaperScissors

How to Play:

The player selects one of three options:

  • Rock (a fist)
  • Paper (an open hand)
  • Scissors (a fist with an index and middle fingers extended, forming a V)

The winner is determined by the rules:

  • Rock crushes Scissors (Rock wins) vs
  • Scissors cuts Paper (Scissors wins) vs
  • Paper covers Rock (Paper wins) vs

If both players choose the same shape, it's a tie, and the game is typically played again. vs
